## Madras Curry

### Overview
A medium-hot, tomato-forward curry with a balanced spice profile and slight tang.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 300–400g meat

### Base
- 2 tbsp oil  
- 1 onion  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 2 tbsp tomato paste  
- 250ml base gravy  

### Spices
- 2 tsp curry powder  
- 1 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp paprika  

---

## Method

### Stage 1 – Base
1. Cook onions until soft.  
1. Add ginger-garlic paste.  
1. Add tomato paste and cook down.

### Stage 2 – Cook
1. Add spices → bloom briefly.  
1. Add meat → fry 3–5 min.  
1. Add base gravy → simmer 15–20 min.

### Stage 3 – Finish
1. Cook until thick and oil separates.

---

## Notes
- Medium heat, not as hot as vindaloo  

---

## Serving
Rice or chapati
