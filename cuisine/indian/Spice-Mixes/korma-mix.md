## Korma Spice Mix

### Overview
A mild, aromatic blend for creamy, nut-based curries.

---

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp coriander powder  
- 1 tsp cumin  
- ½ tsp turmeric  
- ½ tsp cardamom  
- ½ tsp cinnamon  

---

## Method
1. Mix thoroughly.
1. Store airtight.

---

## Notes
- Mild and fragrant  
- No strong heat  

---

## Usage
- Use 1–2 tsp per korma
