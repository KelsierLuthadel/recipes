## Base Curry Powder

### Overview
A balanced, all-purpose curry powder used as the foundation for most BIR-style dishes.

---

## Ingredients
- 2 tbsp coriander powder  
- 1 tbsp cumin powder  
- 1 tbsp turmeric  
- 1 tbsp paprika  
- 1 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp garlic powder  
- 1 tsp ginger powder  

---

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
1. Mix thoroughly until uniform.
1. Store in an airtight container.

---

## Notes
- Mild, balanced, and versatile  
- Forms the backbone of most curries  

---

## Usage
- Use 1–2 tsp per curry as a base layer of flavour
