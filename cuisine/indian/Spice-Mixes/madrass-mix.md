## Madras Spice Mix

### Overview
A medium-hot spice blend with strong chilli and earthy notes.

---

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp base curry powder  
- 1 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp paprika  
- ½ tsp cumin  
- ½ tsp coriander  

---

## Method
1. Mix all spices thoroughly.
1. Store airtight.

---

## Notes
- Medium heat, well-rounded  

---

## Usage
- Use 2–3 tsp per Madras curry
