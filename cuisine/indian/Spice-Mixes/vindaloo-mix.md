## Vindaloo Spice Mix

### Overview
A hot, sharp spice mix designed to complement vinegar-based curries.

---

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp chilli powder  
- 1 tsp cumin  
- 1 tsp coriander  
- ½ tsp black pepper  
- ¼ tsp cloves  
- ¼ tsp cinnamon  

---

## Method
1. Mix all spices together.
1. Store airtight.

---

## Notes
- Designed for heat + acidity  
- Typically combined with vinegar and garlic  

---

## Usage
- Use 2–3 tsp in marinades or curries
