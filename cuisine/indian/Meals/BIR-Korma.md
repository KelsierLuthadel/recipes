## Korma

### Overview
A mild, creamy curry with nutty sweetness and minimal heat.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 300–400g chicken or lamb  

### Base
- 2 tbsp oil  
- 1 onion  
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste  
- 250ml base gravy  

### Sauce
- 3 tbsp cream  
- 2 tbsp ground almonds or cashew paste  
- 1 tsp sugar  

---

## Method

### Stage 1 – Base
1. Cook onions until soft.  
1. Add ginger-garlic paste.

### Stage 2 – Cook
1. Add meat → fry briefly.  
1. Add base gravy → simmer.

### Stage 3 – Finish
1. Add cream, nuts, sugar.  
1. Cook gently until thick.

---

## Notes
- Should be mild and slightly sweet  

---

## Serving
Rice, naan
