## Dhansak Spice Mix

### Overview
A complex blend balancing sweet, sour, and savoury flavours.

---

## Ingredients
- 2 tsp coriander  
- 1 tsp cumin  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 1 tsp paprika  
- ½ tsp cinnamon  
- ½ tsp mustard powder  

---

## Method
1. Mix all spices together.
1. Store in an airtight container.

---

## Notes
- Works with lentils and sweet-sour profiles  

---

## Usage
- Use 2 tsp per dhansak
